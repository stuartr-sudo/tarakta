## What this bot is

A course-faithful Market Maker Method crypto trading bot for Binance futures. Single strategy, 13-gate deterministic pipeline, LLM guardrail (Opus 4.7) as the final filter. Currently paper-trading $100K.

## How it decides to take a trade

Every 5 minutes, for every major crypto pair, the engine runs this pipeline. Setups die at the earliest failing gate:

```
1.  candles_ok             — 1H/4H/1D/15m fetched
2.  formation_found        — M/W or fallback scalp detected on 1H
3.  htf_aligned            — 4H trend-veto (reject counter-trend unless exempt reversal + not accelerating)
4.  level_ok               — post-formation Level 1/2/3 analysis
5.  phase_valid            — inside entry-eligible weekly phase
6.  direction_ok           — weekly bias + FMWB real-move check
7.  target_acquired        — TP tiers computed (+ TP1 distance cap, 3-way-collapse R-multiple fallback)
8.  rr_passed              — R:R ≥ min
9.  scored                 — 16-factor confluence (direction-aware EMA, multi-session bonus, ADR 1.0% tolerance)
10. confluence_passed      — score ≥ min_confluence (35%)
11. retest_passed          — 2+ of 4 retest conditions
12. sanity_agent_passed    — Opus 4.7 LLM veto
13. signal_built           → sizing (1% risk, 50% notional cap, 5% aggregate-risk budget) → execution
```

Commands: [`scripts/trade_audit.py`](../scripts/trade_audit.py) for post-mortem, [`scripts/replay_scan.py`](../scripts/replay_scan.py) for backtest, [`scripts/agent_review.py`](../scripts/agent_review.py) for agent performance.
